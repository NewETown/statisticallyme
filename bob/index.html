Welcome to the Statistically Me demo!

Browse the site as Bob, our willing and able tester.

When a user visits our site they see a personal feed of data visualizations based on a combination of trending topics and what their friends are doing. This is a tech demo, all experiences presented here are static but the flow and feel should give you a good idea of how the site will feel when it's live. To start, scroll down and check out the heatmap of all of the [fake] people who have logged in today.
